# Garden Preferences — 2026

## Household

- Family of 5 (4 home for the summer)

## Location

- Issaquah, Washington (elevation ~680 ft)
- USDA Hardiness Zone: 8b
- Pacific Northwest maritime climate — mild, wet winters and warm, dry summers
- Last frost: typically mid-April
- First frost: typically mid-October
- Growing season: roughly May through September

## Garden Setup

- **2 raised bed garden beds**, each approximately **4 ft × 19.5 ft** (~78 sq ft per bed, ~156 sq ft total)
- **Plot A:** Long side (19.5 ft) faces north. Runs east–west. Trellis along north edge. Rhubarb established at west end.
- **Plot B:** Short side (4 ft) faces north. Runs north–south (perpendicular to Plot A). Garlic already planted at south end (~5 ft of rows). **Sloped** — south end is higher than north end; south side dries out faster.
